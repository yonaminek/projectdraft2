Brain cancer is growth of abnormal cells in your brain. There are multiple types of tumors that can lead to brain cancer. Begign and malignant tumors are the difference between cancerous and non-cancerous cells. Not all brain tumors start in the brain many can be metatstic, and spread to the brain.
